Our Story

Founded in 2010, Brew Haven began as a small coffee cart with a big dream - to bring exceptional coffee and warm hospitality to our community. Today, we're proud to be a beloved neighborhood cafe where every cup tells a story.

We source our beans directly from sustainable farms around the world and roast them in-house to bring out their unique flavors. Our pastries and sandwiches are made fresh daily using locally-sourced ingredients.
